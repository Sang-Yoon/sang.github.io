I am an assistant professor in the School of Computing at KAIST (also affiliated with the Graduate School of AI). Before joining KAIST, I was a research scientist at Adobe Research. I received my Ph.D. from Stanford University, advised by Leonidas Guibas, and received M.S. and B.S. degrees from KAIST. My research focuses on analyzing, synthesizing, and manipulating 3D geometric data based on learning novel neural representations.
My Erdős number is 3 (Minhyuk Sung → Leonidas Guibas → Frances Yao → Paul Erdős).

I am looking for motivated students to join my group. Please reach out to me via email if you are interested in.
